## If a Rotten Worms is infesting an opposing minion, and that minion dies from retaliation, can Absorb take effect on a nearby spire? Or is that not considered being defeated?

It is not considered to be defeated, so cannot be Absorbed.

## Can a unit with "Infest" attack and then infest before retaliation?

You cannot interrupt an attack/retaliation by Infesting in between.
