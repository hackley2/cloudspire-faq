## Can a newly fused Shada minion use talents that trigger after movement? And is there a difference between "after this unit's movement" and "at the end of this unit's movement"?

(summary: No, they can't. And no there kind of isn't)

There is no functional difference between “at the end of movement” and “after movement” (except if both were to come up 
at the same time, end of movement would happen before after movement, but I don’t think that can happen).

After movement means directly after movement. So if a unit fuses, it’s no longer directly after movement even if one of 
the fused units just moved before the fuse. It’s also not technically after this unit’s movement. Just like grouped units, 
its position in its turn picks up where the last unit left off, but that doesn’t mean it’s considered to have done 
whatever the previous unit did. A newly revealed grouped unit could also not do something labelled as “after movement” 
if its predecessor was defeated during its movement. The new unit doesn’t get to move because the previous one did, but 
the new one isn’t considered to have just moved, if that makes sense.

# When a fused Shada appears, does it move? The verbiage in the rulebook is that it "Deploys".

The intention is that it doesn’t move the turn it deploys, and this is also how it was playtested. I look at it as a 
subsequent unit to the minions that fused, so it carries on its turn where they left off, and one of them has already 
moved so it’s already considered to have moved. Similar to if a grouped unit comes into play after the unit on top of 
it has already moved.

## A group of Shada minions move next to a source well. The top one ungroups and moves to the well. Can it immediately fuse with the newly-revealed unit that it was grouped with? Or is that only possible after that unit completes its own separate movement?

"After any other basic Shada minion’s movement” is very intentional wording. It cannot fuse with the unit it just 
ungrouped from. Another unit has to complete its own movement in order to fuse with it.
