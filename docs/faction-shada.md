## Can a newly fused Shada minion use talents that trigger after movement? And is there a difference between "after this unit's movement" and "at the end of this unit's movement"?

(summary: No, they can't. And no there kind of isn't)

There is no functional difference between “at the end of movement” and “after movement” (except if both were to come up at the same time, end of movement would happen before after movement, but I don’t think that can happen).

After movement means directly after movement. So if a unit fuses, it’s no longer directly after movement even if one of the fused units just moved before the fuse. It’s also not technically after this unit’s movement. Just like grouped units, its position in its turn picks up where the last unit left off, but that doesn’t mean it’s considered to have done whatever the previous unit did. A newly revealed grouped unit could also not do something labelled as “after movement” if its predecessor was defeated during its movement. The new unit doesn’t get to move because the previous one did, but the new one isn’t considered to have just moved, if that makes sense.
